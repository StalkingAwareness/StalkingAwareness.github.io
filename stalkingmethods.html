Stalking Methods

There are four common stalking methods that are usually used. They are surveillance, life invasion, interference, and intimidation.

Surveillance

  • Following
  • Watching
  • Waiting
  • Use of tracking software
  • Obtaining info about victim

    • There are many services available online that allow a person to find information on another, like addreses, relations, phone numbers, and more, all available to anyone willing to pay a small fee.

  • Proxy stalking
