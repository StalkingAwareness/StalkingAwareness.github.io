Resources

Victim Advocacy

The Stalking Prevention, Awareness, and Resource Center "helps the helpers", they work to make sure that the victim service providers, campuses, law enforcement agencies, and other places, get the training and resources on how to help and support stalking victims. While they do not directly interact with victims, they have a lot of great resources, like the Stalking Log, and information resources. SPARC: https://www.stalkingawareness.org/

Stop Stalking US is a nationwide nonprofit organization that offer victim-survivor resources and support. Additionally, they have events, information, and offer a place where you can get your story heard: https://www.stopstalkingus.com/

To get victim support resources specific to you state follow this link to the Office for Victims of Crime (OVC): https://ovc.ojp.gov/help-for-victims/help-in-your-state
